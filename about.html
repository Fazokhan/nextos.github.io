About NextOS

NextOS is a custom Android ROM built on the foundation of HyperOS, delivering unmatched stability, reliability, and performance. Our goal is to provide a bloat-free, customizable, and secure Android experience that empowers users to unlock their device’s full potential.

Founded by Faizan Khan, known as FAZO in the community, NextOS is a passion project driven by innovation and user satisfaction. Whether you’re a power user or a casual enthusiast, NextOS is designed for you.
